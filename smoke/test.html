Our process
User experience and content strategy

Step 01

We work and think from abstract to concrete. We like to begin with a broad, big-picture view of a new project and try to define its main objective. Then, everything we create from that point on follows that direction, shaping the project as we go.

Design with a
purpose

Step 02

The creative stage allows us to dig into our user’s journey and craft the art direction for the project. We establish a design system and bridge the gap between brand and digital. We promise to make sure your digital project’s immersive aspects work well in context.

The power of
prototyping

Step 03

We want you, the client, to approve something that’s living. So you can understand the work in context, we move into a prototype phase early on. Putting the prototype in the hands of the client strengthens the relationship.

An agile-inspired
development

Step 04

Our development process breaks down each individual step while considering the project as a whole. We give our best throughout this journey so that your digital project remains an accessible experience and that all the little technical details are dealt with, so that on launch day, the experience will be as user-friendly, and trackable, possible.
